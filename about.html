about me

Hi, my name is Dietmar Thaler. I was born in the beautiful West of Austria.
I was always adventurous and loved traveling. So it happened that after I finished school, I soon went abroad. I started my adventure in London to improve my English skills. Back home, after several years in various workplaces, I started the world tour project. Equipped with a backpack and good shoes, I discovered new parts of the world for myself.

After my return I worked in a small textile company for almost 20 years, where I could do almost everything from Purchasing to Sales and Design.

At the age of 42 I decided to do something completely different and started training as a software developer at Digital Campus Vorarlberg. And that's where I am today.
